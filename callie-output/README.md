# callie-output/

Finished deliverables from Callie — ready for Mollie to review, send, or hand off.

## How it works
- Callie does the build in her own workspace (~/.openclaw/workspace-callie/)
- When done, she drops the finished file here
- Mollie picks it up, reviews, and delivers to Michael or the client

## What lands here

- Brief and copy docs
- Research summaries
- Anything ready to ship
