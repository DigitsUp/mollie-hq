Expiry Points
Points expire on a rolling 365-day window from last order date. This flow activates 90 days before expiry — when there's still time to act — and runs a multi-touch email + SMS sequence to drive a purchase.

Channel Logic
Email + SMS Together
If SMS opted in: send Email → 1 hr delay → send SMS. If not SMS eligible: email only. Never SMS without email pairing.
Primary CTA
Use Your Points
Show exact point balance and its dollar value. Link directly to rewards redemption — not the homepage. Reduce friction at every step.
Exit Condition
Order Placed
Any purchase resets the 365-day clock. Exit the flow immediately on order event. No further sends needed.
Key Message
Urgency + Value
Lead with loss framing: "Your [X] points expire in [N] days." Show dollar equivalent. Final sends escalate urgency significantly.

Tier Structure
Silver / Gold
Silver: $350 spend in last 365 days. Gold: $750 spend in last 365 days. Confirm exact thresholds with Liverpool before launch.
Primary CTA
Shop to Hold Tier
Show exact dollar gap to maintain status. "You need $X more to keep Gold." Direct product link — not homepage. Make the math impossible to ignore.
Exit Condition
Tier Secured
As soon as cumulative spend crosses the threshold, exit immediately. Optionally send a "Tier Secured" confirmation email.
Key Message
Loss Aversion
Frame around what they'll lose — not what they'll gain. Status protection is a stronger motivator than reward. Lead with benefits at risk.

Flow 04 · Celebration
Loyalty Anniversary
Everyone gets their moment. On the 1-year anniversary of a customer's loyalty program join date, we celebrate — and reward it. This happens once per customer, ever. No repeat sends in year two, three, etc.
Trigger Loyalty join date = exactly 365 days ago · One-time per customer · Never repeats
The Reward
Bonus Points Gift
Recommend 100 bonus points auto-credited on the anniversary date. Confirm exact value with Liverpool. Message should feel like a genuine gift, not a promotional blast.
Cadence
1–2 Touch Max
Email on anniversary day. Optional SMS same day with 1hr delay if both channels active. If no engagement after 5 days, one reminder email that points are waiting. Then stop.
Key Message
You've Been Loyal
Highlight total spend, orders, or points earned over the year if data is available. Make it personal. This should feel like a real acknowledgment — not a campaign.
One-Time Flag: On send, tag customer anniversary_sent = true. Flow checks this tag at entry — if present, skip entirely. Ensures no customer ever receives this flow more than once, regardless of re-enrollment logic.
Flow 05 · Suppression
Sunset
5-email sequence targeting contacts dormant 180 days — no open, no click, no site activity. Triggers after win-back exits without re-engagement. Contacts have 60 days to click a single CTA and confirm they want to stay. No purchase required. No response by Day 61 → suppress.
Trigger No email open · No email click · No site activity in 180 days · Contacts that exited win-back without re-engaging

Flow 06 · Channel Expansion
Wallet Pass
Surface-level: a branded referral card in Apple & Google Wallet. Real goal: unlock push notifications as a direct channel. No app download required. Powered by Toki.
Trigger Active loyalty member AND wallet pass not yet installed AND at least 1 order placed
🔔 Push notifications to lock screen
📍 Location-based store alerts
🔗 QR code referral — no app needed
💳 Live points & tier display
⚡ Real-time reward balance updates
🎁 Pass download incentive (bonus pts)
Primary CTA
Add to Wallet
Lead publicly with referral: "Give $25, Get $25." The push notification access is the backend win. Frame it as their VIP loyalty card.
Exit Condition
Pass Installed
Exit on install confirmation. Enroll in push notification segment. Customer is now reachable on a 3rd channel beyond email & SMS.
Cadence
3-Touch Sequence
Email Day 1 → SMS Day 4 (if opted in) → Final email Day 10. Short and feature-forward. This is a channel unlock, not a hard sell.
