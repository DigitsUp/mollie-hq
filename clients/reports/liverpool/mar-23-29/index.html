S4  —  Analysis & Actions
What Improved
  • Flow revenue up 39.5% YoY — the strongest signal in this report. The lifecycle infrastructure is working. $48.3K from flows vs $34.6K same week last year.
  • Welcome Series Email 2026 is the top-performing flow at $9.1K and 57 orders. New series is clearly outperforming last year's welcome sequence.
  • Flows up 2.5% WoW with more recipients (41K vs 39K). Consistent week over week improvement — not a spike.
  • Loyalty SMS showing strong efficiency — $3.8K combined from two small sends (Bronze, Silver/Gold tiers). Revenue per recipient is well above average campaigns.
What Declined
  • Campaign revenue down 49.5% YoY ($30.2K vs $59.7K LY). This is the biggest gap and needs context — last year's same week likely had a higher-volume promotional calendar.
  • Total attributed revenue down 16.8% YoY ($78.4K vs $94.3K). Flows are narrowing the gap significantly but campaigns still need to carry more weight.
  • Email campaign send volume down 22.9% YoY (634K vs 822K recipients). Fewer sends = fewer conversions regardless of efficiency.
  • SMS campaign revenue down 62.6% YoY. Last year's SMS performance in this window was significantly higher — worth investigating what campaigns ran same week LY.
  • NCO Welcome Offer conversion rate was low (21 orders from 48K recipients = 0.044%). Strong list size, weak return — offer or audience alignment needs review.
Recommended Actions
  • Lead with the flow story in tomorrow's call — 39.5% YoY growth is a meaningful win and directly attributable to the work we've done on the lifecycle.
  • Pull last year's campaign calendar for this week and compare send volumes. The YoY campaign gap is almost certainly a volume problem, not an efficiency problem.
  • Review NCO Welcome Offer — 48K recipients with only 21 orders suggests subject line, offer amount, or segment targeting needs adjustment.
  • Loyalty SMS is punching above its weight. Recommend expanding the send window or testing a Gold-tier exclusive send to capitalize on the efficiency signal.
  • Confirm Welcome Series Email 2026 full sequence is active — with 57 orders from email 1 alone, emails 2 and 3 performance should be reviewed for drop-off.
