## Topics

### Problem: Declining Sales & AOV
- Q1 sample flow revenue: $231k → $85k (-63% YoY)
- Campaign AOV: dropped 39% to $62
- Root cause 1: Conservative email strategy (Una + Allie suppressing inactive users) vs. leadership wanting full database sends
  - Proof point: broad sunblock campaign with no purchase/view filter → 291 orders, database is responsive
- Root cause 2: Riversol Plus ($19/mo fee → 10% discount + $19 credit) is cannibalizing spend per order

### Solution: New Flows & Campaigns
**Win-Back Flow (top priority) — 3-Lane Strategy:**
1. Sample Takers — dedicated flow
2. One-Time Buyers — A/B test gift card vs. percent-off promo
3. 2+ Order Customers — warm engagement (no promo) to protect future sales

**Campaign Ideas:**
- SMS Flash Sale — password-protected to drive SMS sign-ups (currently 98% email / 2% SMS)
- Loyalty Tier Nudges — target customers near Gold or Silver tier
- Case Study Email — testimonial-based for sample takers 60+ days out who haven't converted
