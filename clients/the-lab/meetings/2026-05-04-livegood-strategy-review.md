# LiveGood — Strategy Review
**Date:** 2026-05-04
**Source:** Fathom recap (no-reply@fathom.video)
**Meeting:** Review of DigitsUp email strategy and creative proposal for LiveGood

---

## Purpose
Review DigitsUp's email strategy and creative proposal for LiveGood.

## Key Takeaways
- **Strategy Pivot:** LiveGood rejected the initial multi-product Brain Health email — deemed too generic. New directive: focus on a single product (Magnesium) and anchor all messaging to the brand's core story: "highest quality, lowest price, full transparency."
- **Critical Funnel Leak:** Klaviyo analytics revealed major checkout drop-off — 85% cart-to-checkout, but only 64% checkout-to-purchase. Likely caused by a confusing membership auto-billing disclaimer. This is a major immediate revenue opportunity.
- **Data Integrity Issue:** Klaviyo data is incomplete — missing a large portion of LiveGood's 2M+ members. Requires full sync with backend system to enable accurate segmentation.
- **Decision Point:** DigitsUp will create a new Magnesium-focused email mock-up to prove alignment. The May 5th review will determine if the partnership proceeds.

## Topics Covered

### Initial Proposal & Feedback
- DigitsUp presented a "Brain Health" campaign mock-up featuring six products
- LiveGood feedback:
  - Generic messaging — failed to convey brand's unique value proposition
  - Product selection issues (e.g., Collagen misaligned with "brain health" theme)
  - Multiple products create decision paralysis → hurts conversion
- Core brand story must be central: highest quality, lowest prices, full transparency, affordability as mission

### New Strategy: Magnesium-Focused Email
- Product: Magnesium (best-seller, daily essential)
- Offer structure:
  - Primary: Magnesium at $9.95 (member price)
  - Upsell: Daily Essentials Pack (Magnesium + D3 + Multivitamin) for $29.95
- Target audience: Paying members in US, Canada, UK, Australia, NZ

### Technical & Data Issues
- Checkout funnel: 64% completion rate — confusing auto-billing disclaimer likely cause
- Incomplete Klaviyo data — prevents accurate segmentation of full 2M+ member base
- Full data sync required before proper targeting

### Internal Context
- Revenue: LiveGood in a 9-month decline ($5.9M → $3.1M/mo)
- Email currently only 0.4–2% of revenue — massive untapped opportunity
- DigitsUp goal: Increase email revenue to 25% within 90 days
- Phase 1 (60 days): prove concept with Magnesium campaign
