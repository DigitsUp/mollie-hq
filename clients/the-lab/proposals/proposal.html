Automation Strategy

Flows that run
while you coach.

Lifecycle automations are where Klaviyo earns its keep. These are behavior-triggered, personalized sequences — not newsletters. Each one responds to something a real family does in Mindbody and delivers exactly the right message at exactly the right moment.

Free Trial → Membership Conversion Sequence
Trigger: Free trial class booked
The highest-leverage automation in the program. A family books a trial — the relationship starts the moment they confirm. Industry benchmark: 30–50% trial-to-member conversion with a proper nurture sequence.

Member Loyalty

Turn great members
into advocates.

The LAB's community is one of its strongest assets. A structured loyalty program — built inside Klaviyo and tied to Mindbody behavior — rewards the actions that matter most: showing up consistently, referring families, and growing with the gym over time.

🏅
Attendance Milestone Rewards
Track cumulative class visits in Mindbody and reward families at meaningful milestones — 10 classes, 50 classes, 1 year of membership. Makes consistency feel recognized and celebrated, not invisible.
"[Child's name] just hit 50 classes at The LAB. That's real progress — here's something to mark the moment."
🤝
Referral Program
Word of mouth is already a top acquisition channel — a structured referral program gives it a trackable, rewardable spine. Refer a family, earn a free month. Simple, high-conversion, easy to run through Klaviyo.
"Refer a family to The LAB — when they book their first class, you both get a free month. Share your link below."
🎂
Birthday & Anniversary Moments
A child's birthday and a member's LAB anniversary are two of the most personal touchpoints in the year. Automated recognition builds the kind of emotional connection that long-term retention is built on.
"Happy birthday to [child's name] — here's a little something from the LAB family to celebrate."
⭐
VIP Member Tier
Identify your most engaged, longest-tenured families and give them a name. Early camp enrollment access, priority birthday party booking, exclusive events. Makes your best members feel like insiders.
"As a LAB VIP family, you get first access to summer camp enrollment — 48 hours before it opens to the public."
