🔧 Recommended Fixes

1
Suppress Browse/Cart flows for 2–4hrs after welcome send on Day 0 to prevent double-hitting new leads.
2
Add order filter to Welcome #2 and #5 — buyers shouldn't get brand intros or discount reminders post-purchase.
3
Move "Set Your Preferences" email (currently Welcome #3 customer path) into Post-Purchase flow where it belongs.
4
Cap campaign sends to leads <21 days / 0 orders at max 2 per sale event — no AM+PM double sends.
