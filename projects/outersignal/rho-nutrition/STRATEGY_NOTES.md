# Rho Nutrition — Post Purchase Flow Strategy Notes

Source file: rho_post_purchase_strategy.html

## Project Scope
- 18 emails total
- 6 personas
- 3 sends per persona: Day 3, Day 8, Day 21

## Flow Arc
- **Day 3 (Email 1):** Welcome + how to use. Product arrived. Reinforce purchase. Usage instructions. Goal: activation.
- **Day 8 (Email 2):** Product education + results timeline. One week in. Science and belief building. Goal: retention.
- **Day 21 (Email 3):** Reorder + subscription conversion. Dynamic block — subscribers get loyalty message, non-subscribers get subscription value prop. Goal: repeat purchase + subscription conversion.

## 6 Personas
1. **P1 — The High-Performance Executive** (~25%) — CEOs/Founders/VPs. NAD+/cognitive. ROI, optimization, efficiency. Data-led, direct, zero fluff.
2. **P2 — The Creative Entrepreneur** (~15%) — Designers/Artists. Clean/aesthetic. Curated, intentional, creative clarity. Warm, lifestyle-focused.
3. **P3 — The Community Educator and Parent** (~10%) — Teachers/Principals. Family safety/trust. Safe, trustworthy, community. Warm and reassuring.
4. **P4 — The Clinical Wellness Advocate** (~15%) — MDs/Chiropractors/PTs. Bioavailability/science. Clinical, precise, evidence-based. Peer-level tone.
5. **P5 — The Active Performance Optimizer** (~20%) — Trainers/Coaches. Recovery/inflammation. Active, results-focused, coach-like energy.
6. **P6 — The Skilled Trade and Industry Leader** (~15%) — Contractors/Builders. Joint health/energy. No-nonsense, blunt, practical. Straight talk only.

## Brand Voice Guardrails
**Always:** Clean, premium, aspirational. Transformation language. Specific ingredients by name. Liposomal delivery as differentiator. Confident but not hype. Short sentences. White space.
**Never:** Overclaiming health benefits. Discount or urgency tactics. Generic wellness buzzwords. Cluttered copy. Emoji in body copy. Em dashes anywhere.

## Email 3 Dynamic Block Spec
- **Subscriber version:** Acknowledge subscription, reinforce decision, persona-appropriate loyalty message. Light encouragement. CTA to account or product page.
- **Non-subscriber version:** Save X%, never run out, skip/cancel anytime. Persona-appropriate framing. Single clear CTA to subscribe.
- **Klaviyo logic:** Profile property or tag identifies active subscribers. Renders inline — not a separate email. Same subject line, only secondary block changes.
