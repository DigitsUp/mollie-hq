Flow arc
Day 3
Email 1 — Welcome + how to use
Product has arrived or is arriving. Customer is starting their routine. Reinforce the purchase decision, provide clear usage instructions, set expectations for what they will feel and when. Primary goal: activation. Get them using the product correctly from day one.
Day 8
Email 2 — Product education + results timeline
One full week in. Customer has experience with the product. Education lands with context now. Go deeper on the science for clinical and executive personas, lifestyle reinforcement for creative and trade personas. Tell them what is happening in their body. Build confidence in the product working even if they do not feel it yet. Primary goal: retention and belief.
Day 21
Email 3 — Reorder + subscription conversion
One week before a typical 30-day supply runs out. No scramble, no gap in routine. Dynamic block handles the split: subscribers receive a loyalty acknowledgment and encouragement to stay consistent. Non-subscribers receive a subscription value prop with a clear reason to commit. Primary goal: repeat purchase and subscription conversion.
Persona reference
The High-Performance Executive
~25% of customer base
CEOs / Founders / VPs NAD+ / cognitive
Language: ROI, optimization, efficiency, performance edge. Treat health like a business asset. Time-poor. Want proof, not story.
The Creative Entrepreneur
~15% of customer base
Designers / Artists / Owners Clean / aesthetic
Language: curated, clean, intentional, creative clarity. Aesthetics matter. Brand story resonates. Transparency builds trust.
The Community Educator and Parent
~10% of customer base
Teachers / Principals / Nonprofits Family safety / trust
Language: safe, trustworthy, family, community, responsible. Founder mission resonates deeply. They are the family health gatekeeper.
The Clinical Wellness Advocate
~15% of customer base
MDs / Chiropractors / PTs / PAs Bioavailability / science
Language: bioavailability, liposomal delivery, cellular absorption, Glutathione, NAD+. Skeptical until proven. Will recommend to patients if convinced.
The Active Performance Optimizer
~20% of customer base
Trainers / Gym Owners / Coaches Recovery / inflammation
Language: recovery, inflammation, performance, results. Curcumin + Resveratrol. Early adopters. Community influencers.
The Skilled Trade and Industry Leader
~15% of customer base
Contractors / Builders / Logistics Joint health / energy
Language: no-nonsense, potency, results, reliable. Physical wear and tear. Science-backed means not wasting money. Straight talk only.
Brand voice guardrails

Always

  • Clean, premium, aspirational
  • Transformation language
  • Specific ingredients by name
  • Liposomal delivery as differentiator
  • Confident but not hype
  • Short sentences. White space.

Never

  • Overclaiming health benefits
  • Discount or urgency tactics
  • Generic wellness buzzwords
  • Cluttered copy blocks
  • Emoji in body copy
  • Em dashes anywhere

Email 3 dynamic block spec

Subscriber version

Acknowledge they are already subscribed. Reinforce the decision as smart and consistent. Persona-appropriate loyalty message. Light encouragement to stay on track. No hard sell. CTA directs to account or product page.

Non-subscriber version

Lead with the tangible value prop: save X%, never run out, skip or cancel anytime. Persona-appropriate framing on why consistency matters for their specific goals. Single clear CTA to subscribe. No pressure, but clear benefit.

Klaviyo conditional logic: use a profile property or tag to identify active subscribers. Block renders inline within the email — not a separate email. Both versions share the same subject line and primary message. Only the secondary content block changes.
