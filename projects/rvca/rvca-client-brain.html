Critical Issues · Immediate Action
Cart Split Error

🟡 Priority 2 — Welcome Series + Popup Month 2 · Scheduled After Abandonment Suite
Popup / Signup Form — Rebuild
Entry point for Welcome Series Planned ▼
Current state: Basic email capture with no first name field. Generic entry with no personalization capability.

Rebuild plan: Add first name field (unlocks personalization across all flows). Add four world-preference lanes at signup: Surf / Skate / Art / Sport (or "All of it"). World preference routes the subscriber directly into the matching Welcome Series lane — every new subscriber starts a personalized journey from email 1.
Welcome Series — Rebuild
Trigger: Signup via rebuilt popup 4–5 emails · 4 lanes Planned ▼
Current state: 2 emails with a 3-day delay — "a missed handshake." Basic product grid with almost no copy. RVCA's rich brand voice is completely absent.

Rebuild: 4–5 emails across 4 world lanes (Surf / Skate / Art / Sport). Each lane follows the same arc but is differentiated by brand story, team/ANP context, and product angle.

Arc per lane: Brand story → ANP/team context for that world → Product intro → Offer email (timed, lane-specific)
Day 0
Email 1 — Welcome to RVCA
Brand story. What RVCA is. What "Balance of Opposites" means for this world. Personalized to their lane.
Day 2
Email 2 — The Community
ANP artists (Art lane) / team surfers (Surf) / pro skaters (Skate) / fighters (Sport). Who else lives in this world. Real practitioners, not catalog faces.
Day 4
Email 3 — Product Intro
Lane-specific hero products. Lifestyle imagery. In-context, not flat-lay.
Day 7
Email 4 — The Offer
Welcome offer. Quiet, gestural. Not a sale announcement — a welcome gift. Unique code, time-limited.
🔵 Priority 3 — Post-Purchase Months 3–4 · 163K One-Hit Wonders Waiting
Post-Purchase Flow
Trigger: First purchase · Category split 3 phases · 60 days Planned ▼
163K one-hit wonders in the account. Post-purchase is the mechanism that converts single buyers into loyal customers. Category split on purchase category routes to the right sequence.

Category lanes: Surf/Swim · Skate/Street · Sport · Collection
Day 1–7
Phase 1 — The Immediate Window
Brand reinforcement. Care instructions. Community welcome. Confirm they made the right call — don't just confirm the order.
Day 8–30
Phase 2 — Deepen the Relationship
ANP content and team stories relevant to their category. Cross-sell opportunities with genuine behavioral signal. The world, not just the product.
Day 31–60
Phase 3 — Re-Engagement
New arrivals in their category. Loyalty nudge (if loyalty program live). Referral ask from a warm customer who's had time to love the product.
⚪ Future — Winback + Sunset Months 5–6
Winback Flow
Trigger: Lapsed purchaser · 90–180d Planned ▼
Re-engagement series for customers who bought but haven't returned. Sequence and offer strategy TBD based on purchase history and segment analysis at Month 5. Anchor: identity re-connection before any offer.
Sunset Flow
Trigger: Engaged >180d with no action Planned ▼
Remove true inactives from the active billing count. Final re-permission ask before suppression. Keeps the list healthy and improves deliverability for engaged subscribers.
